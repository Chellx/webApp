PLAYDATE: The Handheld Console With A Hand Crank
The Playdate is an upcoming handheld video game console developed by Panic. The console features a black & white screen 2 buttons and a d-pad but what makes this console unique is that it also has a mechanical crank on the side that can be used for gameplay. The console will be released in 2021 with 12 games but new ones will eventually be available through wi-fi in the future. I think this console is very innovative in the use of the crank for unique gameplay mechanics.
